# ONE Menu Customer Differentiation Brief R1

## 1. Traditional IBMS vs VANTARIS ONE

Traditional IBMS helps teams monitor building systems, connected ELV systems, alarms, points, and basic operational states. It is usually system-centric: it shows what is connected, what is alarming, and what the operator can inspect.

VANTARIS ONE is an operational decision and accountability platform. It connects system signals to assets, spaces, faults, work orders, evidence, reports, governance, and customer acceptance. The platform is not limited to showing alarms; it helps teams understand impact, assign responsibility, collect evidence, and prepare accountable handover.

## 2. MMS vs VANTARIS ONE Work Management

Traditional MMS manages maintenance work orders, preventive maintenance schedules, technician assignments, spare parts, and closure records.

VANTARIS ONE Work Management includes those functions as part of a wider operating chain. A fault can be linked to an asset, a work order, spare parts, vendor responsibility, SLA exposure, closure evidence, customer sign-off, and audit trail. Work Management is therefore the execution layer inside a larger platform, not a standalone maintenance database.

## 3. Static ELV Integration Diagram vs ONE Integration & Partner Hub

A static ELV integration diagram shows which source systems connect through which protocols. It is useful for design and handover, but it does not continuously explain operational ownership, data freshness, connector health, protocol evidence, partner SLA exposure, or integration risk.

VANTARIS ONE Integration & Partner Hub treats integration as an operating domain. It tracks source systems, protocol coverage, connector status, partner responsibility, data quality, test evidence, and integration governance. This makes ELV integration observable, accountable, and reusable across industries.

## 4. Asset Registry vs ONE Asset / Space / Point / Evidence Relationship

A traditional asset registry lists equipment, location, type, owner, warranty, and lifecycle information.

VANTARIS ONE keeps that asset lifecycle context, then connects it to site, building, floor, zone, system, point, tag, fault, work order, document, evidence object, and export package. The asset is not just a record; it becomes the operational anchor that explains where a signal came from, who owns it, what work is linked, and what evidence proves closure.

## 5. Industry Solutions vs Platform Core

VANTARIS ONE is cross-industry. Platform core domains such as Dashboard, Command Center, Assets & Locations, Work Management, Faults & Events, Integration & Partner Hub, Reports & Documents, and Governance & Security are reusable across buildings, airports, data centers, malls, industrial facilities, oil and gas plants, and other operating environments.

Industry Solutions are projections of the platform for a specific customer context. For example, Airport uses platform capabilities to organize ELV systems, MMS operations, protocol coverage, service impact, evidence, and handover readiness in airport language. Airport is not a separate platform identity and does not make VANTARIS ONE airport-only.

## 6. Why Similar Objects Appear in Multiple Domains

The same operational object appears in multiple domains because each domain answers a different question:

| Domain | Customer question |
| --- | --- |
| Dashboard | What needs attention first? |
| Command Center | What is happening live and who is handling it? |
| Faults & Events | What was detected, correlated, and diagnosed? |
| Work Management | What needs to be dispatched, executed, closed, and signed off? |
| Assets & Locations | Where is the affected object and how is it related to systems, points, tags, faults, and evidence? |
| Integration & Partner Hub | Which source system, protocol, connector, partner, or gateway is involved? |
| Reports & Documents | What evidence, report, package, or handover artifact can be produced? |
| Governance & Security | Who is allowed, who approved it, what SLA/control applies, and what audit record proves accountability? |
| Industry Solutions | How does this same platform capability appear in an airport, data center, mall, building, plant, or industrial scenario? |

This role-based reuse is intentional. It prevents each team from working in a disconnected system while still letting each team enter from the domain they understand.

## Final Customer Message

Traditional IBMS shows connected systems and alarms.

Traditional MMS manages work orders and preventive maintenance.

VANTARIS ONE connects systems, assets, faults, work orders, evidence, governance, and customer acceptance into one operational decision and accountability platform.
