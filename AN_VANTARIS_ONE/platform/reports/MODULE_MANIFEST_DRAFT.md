# Reports Module Manifest Draft

Reports is the VANTARIS ONE Platform Layer Reporting Workspace (`报表工作台`).

## A1 Scope

Reports A1 is a docs-level module manifest draft only.
This task does not implement backend, frontend, runtime, API, DB, or schema.

## Future Consumption Scope

Reports will consume standardized references and metadata for:

- events, alarms, and faults
- maintenance and SLA contexts
- sustainability and operations contexts
- evidence-linked reporting contexts
- audit/governance and module readiness/status contexts

## Boundary Rules

- Reports does not directly connect to external systems.
- Reports does not directly connect to EDGE/LINK/DB runtime.
- Reports does not own Contracts schema.
- Reports does not create real PDF/Excel/export runtime.
