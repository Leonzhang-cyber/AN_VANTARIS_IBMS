# ESG A0 Metric Model

## 1. Energy Metrics

- total electricity consumption
- electricity consumption by site/building/zone
- peak demand, future
- energy intensity
- equipment energy contribution, future

## 2. Utility Metrics

- water consumption
- gas consumption
- chilled water consumption
- utility consumption by building/zone

## 3. Carbon Metrics

- estimated carbon emission
- carbon intensity
- carbon factor reference
- avoided emission, future
- maintenance-related carbon impact, future

## 4. Sustainability KPIs

- energy baseline variance
- energy anomaly count
- alarm/event energy impact count
- maintenance impact on energy performance
- report readiness status

## 5. Reporting Boundary

ESG A0 defines metric boundaries only.
It does not create a real calculation engine.
It does not create a formal compliance filing module.
It does not modify DB/API/frontend.
